Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your Remero account and associated data in the app:

Settings > Delete Account

If you cannot access the app, contact us at support@meroviq.com and request account deletion.

For details about what data is deleted or retained, see:

  • Privacy Policy
  • Terms of Service

계정 및 데이터 삭제

Remero 앱에서 계정과 관련 데이터를 삭제할 수 있습니다:

Settings > Delete Account

앱에 접근할 수 없는 경우 support@meroviq.com 으로 계정 삭제를 요청할 수 있습니다.
